# Jellycut Studios — Blog Content Strategy
> Saved: July 16, 2026 | Goal: Attract new clients from India & Abroad

---

## The Core Formula (Apply to Every Post)

Every blog post must:
1. **Target a specific search query** a potential client actually types
2. **Demonstrate specific expertise** (not generic "AI tips")
3. **Reference a real case study** from the portfolio where possible
4. **End with a natural CTA** to start a project
5. **Include an AEO FAQ section** optimised for Google's AI Overviews

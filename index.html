Yes. Jellycut Studios is a fully remote, async-first studio. We work with clients in the US, UK, Australia, Canada, and across Europe. All communication is async — no timezone calls required. We cover US and UK working hours through async updates.

No. You start by filling out a short intake form — takes about 15 minutes. We use that brief to produce your first draft. You review it on a shared preview link. No calls required at any stage unless you specifically want one.

What is vibe-coding and what kind of apps does Jellycut build?
Vibe-coding is AI-assisted software development where large language models and AI tools handle repetitive code, allowing human designers and developers to focus on creative decisions and user experience. Jellycut Studios builds web apps, SaaS dashboards, landing pages, interactive portfolios, and tools — shipping in weeks instead of months.
